// FAQ

Common questions

How do I join Geek Room?
Fill out the contact form above or attend one of our induction events. Membership drives happen at the start of each semester.
Is Geek Room only for CS students?
Not at all! We welcome students from all branches — ECE, ME, CE, IT. If you love tech, you belong here.
Are events free to attend?
Most workshops and talks are free for members. Hackathons may have a small nominal registration fee to cover logistics.
Can companies collaborate with Geek Room?
Absolutely! We work with companies for workshops, hackathon sponsorships, and guest speaker sessions. Email us to discuss.
